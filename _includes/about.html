I am a postdoctoral research associate at the National Institute for Mathematical and Biological Synthesis. My research interests are forest management and remote sensing. My main focus is national-scale forest inventory analysis in the U.S. South and Japan using remote sensing data and field inventory data. In my research I intensively use R, Python and Google Earth Engine. In my free time I enjoy hiking, cooking and running.
